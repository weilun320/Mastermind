How To Play?

Click here to skip
  1. First, Mastermind will randomly pick a sequence of colors from the eight colors above. The number of colors is the number of pins. The default number of pins is 4 but you may change at Gamemode and save it.

  2. The goal is you must guess the exact positions of the colors that Mastermind has picked.

  3. By default, a color will only appear once in the sequence. If you have turned on Duplicate option, then any color will be repeated any number of times in the sequence.

  4. Drag & drop colors to the empty pins. Click on Check button after filling up all pins and the game will response with the result of your guess.

  5. For each correct color and correct position from your guess that matches the sequence, a small green pin will be displayed on the right side of current guess.

  6. For each correct color from your guess but Incorrect position from the sequence, a small red pin will be displayed on the right side of current guess.

  7. You win and earn scores when you guess the correct colors and right position of the sequence in 10 tries.
